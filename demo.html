Guided Scenario

One confusing payment, made understandable

The strongest demo is not every feature. It is a real reconciliation problem becoming understandable and actionable.

💳

Before Fincline

A payment lands in the bank feed with a weak reference. The amount looks slightly off, the payer name is not clean, and the user now has to check multiple systems to understand it.

1
Transaction appears ACH credit posts with incomplete memo data.
2
Amount does not cleanly match Could be a fee deduction, partial payment, or split payment.
3
Manual detective work begins Bank feed, accounting system, and email all need to be checked.
🧾

With Fincline

Fincline assembles the context before the user has to chase it. The workflow shows what likely happened, why that conclusion was reached, and what the next best action is.

1
Candidate invoices are ranked Amount, timing, customer history, and references are evaluated together.
2
Reasoning stays visible The user sees whether the item looks exact, partial, net-of-fee, or uncertain.
3
The right path is obvious Confirm quickly if strong, or investigate with context if not.
