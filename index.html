The Problem

Why this work still takes too long

A payment hits the bank, but it does not clearly say what invoice it belongs to. So someone has to figure it out by hand. That is where the time goes.

1

Payments come in with missing details

ACH, wires, payouts, and settlements often arrive with short memos, unclear references, or no invoice number at all.

2

Teams have to play detective

Bookkeepers bounce between the bank, accounting system, inbox, and spreadsheets just to understand who paid and what was covered.

3

The hard items pile up near close

Partial payments, split payments, grouped deposits, and net-of-fee payouts get pushed aside until they become close blockers.

How It Works

Fincline helps connect the dots

Instead of making your team search across systems, Fincline brings the important signals together so payment review becomes faster and simpler.

1

See incoming payment activity in one place

Fincline connects to your bank, accounting system, and inbox so your team can review payment activity without chasing information across tools.

2

Get suggested matches with context

Fincline suggests likely matches and explains why a payment belongs to an invoice, a group of invoices, or a review queue.

3

Review faster and post with confidence

Routine items move forward faster. The uncertain ones go into review so your team spends time where judgment is actually needed.
