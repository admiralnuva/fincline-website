The Problem

Why reconciliation still breaks

Payments lose context as they move from payment rail to bank feed to accounting system. By the time your team sees the transaction, the information they need to reconcile it is often gone.

A

ACH or wire with no usable reference

A payment arrives with a vague memo or no memo at all. Your team digs through invoices, customer history, and email just to figure out who paid.

N

Net settlement amounts that look wrong

Processor fees, BNPL settlements, and deductions make valid payments look like mismatches when the accounting system sees only the net number.

P

Partial, split, and installment payments

One invoice may be paid across multiple transactions, or one payment may cover several invoices. Simple amount-and-date rules break fast.
