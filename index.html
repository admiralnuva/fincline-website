How it works

Four stages from upload to close

Fincline follows your existing close workflow — it just does the matching and reasoning work for you.

1
Connect

Upload payments

Connect your bank or upload statements and payment processor files. Transactions extracted and classified automatically.

2
Import

Upload invoices

Connect QuickBooks or upload receivable and payable invoices manually. PDF, Excel, and CSV supported.

3
Review

Review matches

Rules engine suggests matches. Fincline Agent explains each one. Confirm high-confidence matches and flag exceptions.

4
Close

Export and close

Confirmed matches move to the reconciliation queue. Push to QuickBooks or export manually. Never writes until you say so.
