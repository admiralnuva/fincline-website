What's Included

Everything your bookkeeper
needs. Nothing they don't.

⇄

AI Payment Matching

Fuzzy matching connects ACH payments, wires, and checks to the right invoice — even when references are missing or vendor names don't match exactly.

📧

AP Bill Capture from Email

Vendor invoices that arrive as PDF attachments are automatically parsed and pushed to QuickBooks as draft bills. No manual data entry.

📤

AR Invoice Tracking

Know exactly which invoices have been opened, which haven't, and which are overdue. Auto follow-up emails go out at 7, 14, and 30 days.

⚑

Duplicate & Fraud Alerts

Fincline automatically flags duplicate payments, unknown senders, and suspicious patterns before they become costly problems to unwind.

📊

QuickBooks & Xero Sync

Two-way sync keeps your accounting software up to date automatically. Confirmed matches apply instantly. No double handling.

🔒

Read-Only & Secure

Fincline never moves money. Bank access is read-only via Plaid. Email access is read-only OAuth. Your data stays yours.
