The Workflow

How Fincline works step by step

Fincline follows the same path your team already uses, but with more context, better prioritization, and less manual investigation.

Step 1

Connect your systems with read-only access

Start by connecting the bank, accounting system, and inbox. Fincline uses read-only connections to analyze transactions and supporting context before any action is taken.

  • Bank activity is synced into one view
  • Invoice and customer data are available for matching
  • Email and remittance signals add missing context
Step 2

Classify payment behavior before matching

Fincline identifies whether a payment looks exact, partial, split, grouped, net-of-fees, or otherwise unusual before recommending how it should be handled.

  • Separates routine items from edge cases early
  • Recognizes real-world payment patterns that break simple rules
  • Reduces time wasted on false mismatches
Step 3

Suggest likely matches with reasoning

Instead of showing only a mismatch, Fincline explains what it thinks happened, how confident it is, and what rule or pattern influenced the recommendation.

  • Confidence scores help teams prioritize quickly
  • Reasoning shows why the match was suggested
  • Custom rules reflect how your business actually operates
Step 4

Route work into the right review state

High-confidence items move into ready-to-confirm workflows, while uncertain matches are separated into needs-review, exception, or investigation queues.

  • Routine work moves faster
  • Uncertain items are easier to investigate
  • Teams stop treating every transaction as equally urgent
Step 5

Confirm only when your team is ready

Fincline is designed for control. Analysis comes first. Write-back happens only after explicit user confirmation, with the decision captured for auditability.

  • Read-only until review is complete
  • Matches can be explained before action
  • Audit trails preserve trust and accountability
Step 6

Keep backlog from turning into month-end pain

Queued work that was not reviewed right away does not disappear. Fincline keeps it organized by status, age, and period so teams can clear backlog without losing context.

  • Pending items stay visible and structured
  • Older work can be prioritized safely
  • Month-end surprises are reduced
