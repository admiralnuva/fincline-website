Connect once.

Here's exactly what happens from the moment you sign up to the moment your bookkeeper gets their mornings back.

1

Connect your accounts

One-time setup · Under 8 minutes · Read-only access only

🏦

Your bank via Plaid

Fincline uses Plaid — the same technology used by millions of apps — to connect to your business bank account. Read-only. Fincline never moves money.

📊

QuickBooks or Xero

OAuth connection to your accounting software. Fincline imports your open invoices and customer list. Confirmed matches sync back automatically.

✉️

Gmail or Outlook

Read-only OAuth access to your inbox. Fincline scans for vendor invoices and remittance emails. It never sends email on your behalf without permission.

✅

That's it — you're live

From this point, Fincline runs quietly in the background. Your bank syncs in real time. QuickBooks syncs every 15 minutes. Email scans every 5 minutes.

2

Fincline matches payments to invoices

Runs overnight · AI-powered · 94% accuracy

⚡

Bank transaction arrives

An ACH, wire, or check clears your bank. Fincline picks it up instantly via Plaid webhooks — amount, sender, reference, timestamp.

🤖

AI scores every possible match

Fincline compares the payment against every open invoice — fuzzy vendor name matching, amount, reference number, remittance emails — and assigns a 0–100 confidence score.

📧

Email context adds confidence

If a remittance email arrived before the payment, Fincline pre-matches them. This alone pushes most matches above 90% — straight to auto-apply.

📋

AP bills captured in parallel

While matching bank payments, Fincline is also reading your inbox — extracting vendor, amount, due date, and invoice number from every PDF attachment. Straight into QuickBooks as a draft bill.

🔍 Fincline never guesses. Every match has a confidence score and a reason. If it's not sure, it flags it for you — it never auto-applies a low-confidence match.
3

You review in 10 minutes

One dashboard · One click to confirm · QuickBooks updates automatically

☀️

Open Fincline each morning

Everything that happened overnight is waiting for you. Auto-matched transactions are already in QuickBooks. You only see the ones that need a human decision.

👆

One click to confirm edge cases

For the 6% of transactions Fincline isn't sure about, it shows you its best match and why. You confirm or reassign with a single click — it applies to QuickBooks immediately.

📤

AR invoices tracked automatically

Fincline shows you which invoices have been opened, which haven't, and which are overdue. Auto follow-up emails go out on your behalf at 7, 14, and 30 days.

🚨

Alerts catch what humans miss

Duplicate payments, unknown senders, and partial payments are flagged before they become problems. Most customers catch at least one duplicate in their first month.

Security & Privacy

Built for trust from day one

Your financial data deserves more than a checkbox. Here's exactly how Fincline protects it.

🔒

Read-only bank access

Fincline connects via Plaid with read-only permissions. It can never initiate transfers, move money, or modify your bank account in any way.

📧

Read-only email access

Gmail and Outlook access is OAuth read-only. Fincline reads invoice attachments only. It cannot send emails on your behalf without explicit configuration.

🏛

Encrypted at rest and in transit

All data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Your accounting data is never shared with third parties.
